Our Story

BAGORA was born in 2020 in a small workshop in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, with just three craftspeople and one mission — to make premium-quality bags accessible to everyone in India.

We noticed a gap in the market: beautiful, durable bags were either too expensive or poorly made. We set out to change that, spending months sourcing the finest materials, refining designs, and perfecting construction techniques.

Today, BAGORA serves over 50,000 customers across India, offering everything from everyday backpacks to stylish travel bags — each made with the same care and passion as that very first bag.
